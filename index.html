How It Works

Wave on Pulse combines the energy of live radio with the permanence of the PulseChain blockchain.

📡

1. Live Broadcast

We go live on X Spaces, Rumble, or other platforms. Raw conversations and alpha in real time.

✍️

2. Transcription

Our team transcribes the best parts of each show. Currently human-assisted for quality.

⛓️

3. On-Chain Publishing

The content is permanently written to PulseChain using Pulse Underground — immutable and censorship-resistant.

Current Reality: Hybrid system — Human energy on the front end, fully decentralized backend on PulseChain.

Future Goal: Full automation with real-time transcription and direct on-chain publishing.
