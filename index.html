How It Works

Wave on Pulse combines the energy of live radio with the permanence of the PulseChain blockchain.

📡

1. Live Broadcast

We go live on X Spaces, Rumble, or other platforms. This is where the real conversations, debates, and alpha happen — raw and unfiltered.

✍️

2. Transcription

Our team transcribes the most valuable parts of the show into clean text. This step is currently human-assisted to maintain high quality.

⛓️

3. On-Chain Publishing

The transcript (or key segments with timestamps) is written permanently to the PulseChain blockchain using Pulse Underground — our serverless, censorship-resistant protocol.

Current Reality: Hybrid system.
Human energy drives the live shows and transcription today, while the final record lives fully decentralized and immutable on PulseChain.

Future Goal: Full automation — real-time transcription and direct on-chain publishing.
